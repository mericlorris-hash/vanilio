The Odoo Shopify Connector is a powerful integration solution that seamlessly connects your Shopify ecommerce store with Odoo . It eliminates manual data entry and operational bottlenecks by synchronizing key business data - including products, inventory, orders, customers, pricing, and fulfillment between Odoo and Shopify in real time or on a scheduled basis. This means you can manage your entire commerce and back-office workflows from one central system, reducing errors, improving efficiency, and freeing yourself or your team to focus on growth rather than repetitive tasks.

Businesses using the connector benefit from unified inventory control, automatic order processing, consolidated customer data, and real-time visibility into sales performance - all without switching back and forth between platforms. It supports multi-store setups and offers configurable synchronization rules so you can tailor the integration to your operational needs.

Does the Shopify Odoo Connector allow two-way synchronization of Products?
Yes, you can export Products from Odoo to Shopify as well as import Products from Shopify to Odoo. If the products are already present on Odoo as well as Shopify, you can establish a synchronization between them using the connector.
How many Shopify stores can I connect with a single Odoo Instance?
The Shopify Odoo Connector lets connect as many Shopify stores as you want with a single Odoo Instance.
On what basis are the product synchronized?
The connector synchronizes products by matching the Internal Reference field in Odoo with the SKU field in Shopify. It is mandatory to have a unique SKU or Internal Reference for a product.
How do I manage product prices with the connector?
The connector allows you to update price from Odoo to Shopify via a pricelist. You can set up a pricelist per Shopify instance in Odoo and update the price to Shopify via the pricelist. You also import the prices from Shopify to Odoo, however, the prices will get imported to the pricelist and not directly to the product.
How do I manage stock updates with the connector?
The connector allows you to update stock from Odoo to Shopify either manually or automatically via a cron job. Stock levels get automatically adjusted in Odoo based on the imported orders. You can also import stock levels from Shopify to Odoo. The imported stock values will create an inventory adjustment entry in Odoo which upon validating will affect the actual stock levels. In the connector settings, you can choose one warehouse per Shopify Instance from which the stock is to be managed.
What kind of orders get imported to Odoo and how can I control it?
Any order on Shopify can be imported to Odoo if the connector has been configured correctly. The connector provides a configuration that lets you choose if you want to import only unfulfilled orders or fulfilled orders or both.
Do I need the SSL certificate in my Odoo for Webhook operations?
Yes. Your endpoint must be an HTTPS webhook address with a valid SSL certificate that can correctly process the event.
I am using Webhooks. Does it have any prerequisites?
If you want to use Webhooks, you must enable the database filter. However, in the case of Odoo.sh, it is handled automatically.
Does your connector support Payout reports in the Odoo Community version?
No
Why are products with more than 3 attributes not exported to Shopify?
Shopify has a platform-level limitation that allows a maximum of three attributes (also known as options) per product. Due to this restriction, any product with more than three attributes cannot be exported to Shopify. This restriction is specific to Shopifys handling of product variants and is not related to the connector or Odoo.
